Sample Folders generate the labels for the whole Pipeline. mind the naming.

-Following the scheme is recommended. If you dont follow you might have to manually define short_labels and abbreviations as labels for the bar and polar plots. (edit the Dictionaries in load_default_params)



________________

Scheme:

"""""""
Substituent position- substituent- isotope-Backbone 
-> as in "4-Amino-14N-TEMPO"

(use uppercase letters)

"""""""""



	Substituent Position: usually 4 for TEMPO, 3 for cp

	Substituent: 	NH2 = "Amino"
		=O  = "Oxo"
		COOH= "Carboxy"
		OH  = "Hydroxy"

	isotope: "14N" or "15N"

	Backbone: {TEMPO : Tetramethylpiperidin-N-Oxide,
	  proxyl: pyrrolidin-N-Oxide}  	
"""""""

exceptions: 

	-For Oxo71 etc. append "Trityl". This facilitates skipping of the calculation of coupling constants for trityls.
